Teaching strategies using active-learning

We have developed teaching resources for engineering instructors:

  • Videos, slides and activity constructors to design active learning technqiues such as Peer-instruction and Think-Pair-Share.
  • Videos and slides from Teach 10000 Teachers (T10KT) workshop on 'Pedagogies for Effective Use of ICT in Engineering Education'

Research resources for educational technology PhD students

We have developed resources for ET researchers, particularly PhD students:

  • Guidelines and templates for planning, conducting and reporting ET research studies
  • Videos and slides on 'Research Methods in ET' from T10KT workshop
  • Videos and slides on 'How to read a research paper' and 'Tips on lit review' from T10KT workshop on Research Methods
  • Slides on 'Creating your elevator pitch', from HS791 course on Technical Communication

Project OSCAR

300+ Interactive animations and simulations developed for various tertiary level topics in science and engineering domains.
